Cosplash: something made awesome by GOODCOPY.

Cosplash is a side project brought to life because words are my vice and photos make me write them. Inspired by Crew's Unsplash and their mantra to "make something awesome", Cosplash combines my love of words and photography by taking one and conceptualising it into another. I hope Cosplash captures Crew's attention because I'd love to work for Crew.
